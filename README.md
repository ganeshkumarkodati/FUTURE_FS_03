## 📝 Project Overview
Godavari Vindu is a modern, responsive website built for a local authentic restaurant in Tanuku. The platform serves as a digital storefront to showcase the restaurant's traditional culinary heritage, featuring a categorized menu and an integrated table reservation system to improve customer experience and streamline booking operations.

## 🚀 Business Pitch (How this helps the business grow)

**1. The Problem:**
Local authentic restaurants often rely heavily on foot traffic and word-of-mouth. While Godavari Vindu serves incredible regional food, the lack of a professional digital storefront means they miss out on travelers, advance table reservations, and younger demographics searching for the "best traditional food near me."

**2. The Solution:**
I developed a 24/7 digital concierge for the restaurant. The website features high-quality visuals to trigger appetite, a fully categorized digital menu to showcase the vast Godavari cuisine (Tiffins, Non-Veg, Sweets), and an integrated reservation system that reduces phone-line congestion during peak dining hours.

**3. The Business Value:**
By launching this platform, Godavari Vindu will:
* **Capture more advance bookings** through the frictionless reservation form.
* **Improve local SEO visibility**, making it easier for tourists and locals to find their exact menu offerings online.
* **Establish a premium brand presence** that matches the high quality of their traditional food, ultimately driving increased revenue and long-term customer loyalty.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
* **HTML5:** Semantic structure and content organization.
* **CSS3:** Custom styling, responsive grid layouts, and CSS variables for consistent theming.
* **JavaScript (Vanilla):** DOM manipulation, sticky navigation effects, and form submission handling.
